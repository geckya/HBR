HOUSTON BABYWEARING RESOURCE

Non-profit organization offering free public meetings across the Greater Houston area, teaching safe and comfortable use of baby carriers

About Us

HBR is a non-profit organization offering free public meetings teaching safe and comfortable use of baby carriers

We maintain a library of carriers available for trial, fitting, and to 'see what's out there'

$10 and $30 Memberships available to take carriers home on a monthly basis

Get help with your carrier, or learn about a new one!

Come to learn, play, and socialize!

Children of all ages and all caregivers of children welcome

Visit our Facebook page for meeting schedule/locations and more info
